WHO ARE YOU
Started in 2016, the YourMasterpieces community now has over 20K artists across 142 countries and grows stronger every day.
YourMasterpieces is the first and only social platform for the non-digital fine art community with a vision to help artists to reach a global audience both digitally and tangibly. Artists, art collectors, art galleries, art educators and everyone who loves art have joined the platform.>
